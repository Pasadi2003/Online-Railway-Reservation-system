Best care Animal hospital was established in October 2023 aiming to provide better and professional service for all the pet owners. Hospital is fully equipped with modern technology, professional qualified team of doctors, experienced technical personnel and professionally trained team of Veterinary Assistants and Dog Handlers to offer you a good service.
Our services include OPD treatments & vaccination, Spay/ Neuter and other surgeries, Grooming, Dental Care, Pet Boarding & Hospitalization, Pet Taxi & Mobile Veterinary Service, Laboratory facilities, Digital X-ray, Scanning etc.
With our Pet Boarding & Hospitalization facility we are helping the pet owners to plan their vacation without worrying about safety of their pet and would give hospitalized treatments when they are sick.
To provide a better service to the community, we can conduct mobile spay neuter programs in your neighborhood, private and government organizations. We have conducted large and small-scale sterilization and vaccinations campaigns around the country in collaboration with other animal welfare organizations in Sri Lanka throughout these years. With the field mobile unit our main aim is to safeguard your community from rabies and to reduce the increasing number of homeless puppies.
